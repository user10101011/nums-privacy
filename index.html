Privacy Policy

Nums: Daily Angel Numbers

This page explains how Nums: Daily Angel Numbers handles information when you use the app.

Effective date

March 19, 2026

Nums: Daily Angel Numbers ("the App") is provided by Aleksandr Bakhmut ("we", "us", or "our").

1. Overview

Nums: Daily Angel Numbers is an informational mobile app that lets users explore number meanings, view a daily number, save favorite numbers, search numbers, use a widget, receive optional reminders, and share content.

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not sell your personal information.

2. Information We Store on Your Device

The App stores certain information locally on your device in order to provide app functionality. This may include:

  • saved or favorite numbers
  • recent search queries
  • daily number state, including whether a daily number has been revealed
  • widget-related state shared between the app and its widget
  • app language preferences and related local settings

This information is stored locally using iOS system storage such as UserDefaults, App Group storage, and Keychain.

3. Photos Permission

If you choose to save a generated image from the App to your device, the App may request permission to add images to your photo library.

We only access this permission when you choose to use the save-to-photos feature.

4. Notifications

The App may request permission to send local notifications, such as reminders related to the daily number feature.

Notifications are optional. You can allow or deny them, and you can change your preference at any time in your device settings.

5. Sharing Features

If you choose to share content from the App, the App may pass text or images to apps or services you select, such as Messages, Mail, WhatsApp, Telegram, X, or Instagram Stories.

If you share to Instagram Stories, the App may temporarily place image data on the system pasteboard so Instagram can receive it.

Any information you choose to share is then handled by the third-party app or service you selected, under that third party's own terms and privacy policy.

6. Network Requests

The App may make a limited network request to obtain a trusted server time used for app functionality related to daily content timing.

At the time of writing, this request may be sent to https://www.google.com/generate_204.

When this happens, the destination server and your network provider may automatically receive technical information such as your IP address, request metadata, and standard device or network information needed to complete the request.

We do not use this request for advertising, profiling, or analytics.

7. Data Collection by Us

We do not use the App to create user accounts.

We do not knowingly collect personal information for our own analytics or advertising purposes through the App.

We do not use third-party advertising SDKs in the App.

We do not track you across apps or websites for advertising purposes.

8. Data Retention

Information stored locally on your device remains there until:

  • you delete it in the App, where applicable
  • you remove the App or widget
  • your device or system clears the data
  • you revoke permissions or reset device settings

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Security

We use standard platform-provided storage mechanisms offered by iOS. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will post the updated version at the Privacy Policy URL associated with the App and update the effective date above.
